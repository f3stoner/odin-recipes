Lasagna

Description

This dish was made for those cool nights when you are craving something warm and hearty. The layers of noodles, cheese, sauce, and meat combine together to make every bite like a mini vacation to Italy.

By using whole fresh ingredients, the flavors will truly pop in this dish. Make sure to source locally if at all possible.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Sausage
